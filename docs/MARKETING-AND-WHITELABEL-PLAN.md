# Marketing & White-Label Plan — SPD Cert Prep

## Part 1: Traffic & Lead Generation Marketing Plan

### Target Audience

**Primary:** Sterile processing technicians preparing for CRCST, CHL, or CER exams — often working 10–12 hour shifts in under-resourced departments, studying nights and weekends.

**Secondary:** SPD department managers, educators, and healthcare facilities looking to certify their workforce.

---

### Channel Strategy

#### 1. SEO & Organic Search (Highest ROI, 3–6 month horizon)

These are the highest-intent buyers — people actively searching for help passing their exam.

**Target keywords:**
- `CRCST practice test` / `CRCST exam prep`
- `CHL certification study guide`
- `CER practice questions`
- `how to pass CRCST exam`
- `HSPA exam prep 2026`
- `sterile processing technician certification`

**Actions:**
- Add a `/blog` section with articles targeting long-tail keywords:
  - "CRCST Exam Domain Breakdown: What You Need to Know"
  - "AAMI ST79 Explained for Sterile Processing Techs"
  - "CHL vs CRCST: What's the Difference and Which to Take First"
  - "How to Study for the CRCST Exam While Working Full Time"
- Add structured data (FAQ schema) to exam pages so Google shows answer snippets
- Build a free `/crcst-domain-overview` landing page with real study content — captures organic traffic and converts to sign-ups
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console immediately

**Estimated cost:** $0–$200/month (time or a freelance writer)

---

#### 2. Facebook & Reddit Communities (Free, Immediate)

SPD professionals are highly active in niche communities. This is the fastest path to first users.

**Facebook Groups to join and participate in (not spam):**
- "Sterile Processing Professionals" (large, active)
- "CRCST Study Group"
- "Central Service Technician Network"
- "HSPA Members"

**Reddit:**
- r/sterileprocessing
- r/HealthcareWorkers

**Strategy:**
- Answer questions genuinely — mention the platform only when directly relevant
- Share free resources (domain breakdowns, study tips) that link back to the site
- Post a "I built this tool for SPD techs" launch post with a free trial offer

**Estimated cost:** $0

---

#### 3. TikTok / Instagram Reels (Medium-term, 30–60 days)

Short-form video is underutilized in this niche and can reach a large audience quickly.

**Content ideas:**
- "What's actually on the CRCST exam? (domain breakdown)"
- "I failed my CRCST. Here's what I wish I knew."
- "AAMI ST79 in 60 seconds"
- "Day in the life of a sterile processing tech"
- Speed-run a practice question on screen with explanation

**Posting cadence:** 3–5 videos/week, first 60 days
**Goal:** 1–2 viral clips that drive profile traffic → link in bio → site sign-ups

**Estimated cost:** $0 (phone + screen recording)

---

#### 4. YouTube (Long-term asset, 60–90 day horizon)

YouTube ranks in Google. A library of SPD exam content will compound over time.

**Video series ideas:**
- "CRCST Exam Prep Series" — one video per domain (24 domains = 24 videos)
- "CHL Study Guide" — leadership and management concepts
- "Ask the AI" — screen-record AI chat sessions answering tough SPD questions

**Estimated cost:** $0–$100/month (basic editing tools)

---

#### 5. Email List (Start Day 1)

Every visitor who doesn't sign up immediately should be captured via email.

**Lead magnet options:**
- "Free CRCST Domain Checklist" (PDF)
- "Top 20 CRCST Practice Questions" (PDF)
- "HSPA Exam Prep Roadmap" (email sequence)

**Nurture sequence (5 emails over 10 days):**
1. Welcome + free resource delivery
2. "The #1 mistake CRCST test-takers make"
3. Domain mastery explainer + platform feature highlight
4. Student success story / testimonial
5. Limited-time offer or call to action to upgrade

**Tools:** MailerLite or Brevo (both have free tiers up to 1,000 contacts)

**Estimated cost:** $0–$25/month

---

#### 6. LinkedIn (B2B + Professional Credibility)

LinkedIn reaches managers, educators, and HR decision-makers — essential for the B2B white-label play.

**Content strategy:**
- Post weekly: SPD industry insights, certification stats, patient safety data
- Share student success stories (with permission)
- Publish long-form articles on SPD workforce development
- Connect with: SPD managers, hospital HR, nursing educators, healthcare L&D leads

**Estimated cost:** $0

---

#### 7. Paid Ads (Activate after organic validation)

Only invest in paid ads once organic channels confirm which messaging converts.

**Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram):**
- Target: Healthcare workers, ages 22–45, interests in sterile processing / HSPA / hospital work
- Ad creative: "787 practice questions. AI study chat. Pass your CRCST."
- Retarget site visitors who didn't sign up
- Budget to test: $300–$500/month

**Google Search Ads:**
- Bid on: `CRCST practice test`, `sterile processing exam prep`
- These keywords have high commercial intent
- Budget to test: $200–$400/month

---

#### 8. Partnerships & Affiliates

**HSPA local chapters:** Reach out to chapter leaders to mention the platform in newsletters or meetings — offer a group discount code.

**SPD educators & instructors:** Offer a free instructor account in exchange for recommending to students.

**Resume partnership (already exists with Qualified Resume):** Cross-promote more aggressively — every user who passes should be funneled to the resume service.

**Healthcare staffing agencies:** Agencies placing SPD techs want certified candidates — offer co-marketing.

## Part 2: White-Label Plan for Schools & Hospitals

### What White-Labeling Means Here

A white-label version allows a hospital, vocational school, or training organization to offer this platform under **their own brand** — their logo, colors, domain (e.g., `prep.generalhospital.com`), and potentially their own question sets — while the underlying technology is powered by this platform.

---

### Why This Is a Strong B2B Opportunity

- Hospitals are required by The Joint Commission and CMS to maintain certified SPD staff
- Turnover in SPD departments is high — facilities constantly onboard and recertify techs
- Schools offering sterile processing vocational programs need exam prep tools
- Facilities currently buy physical textbooks ($60–$120/ea) or pay for expensive in-person training
- A white-label SaaS solution is far cheaper and more measurable

---

### White-Label Tier Model

#### Tier 1 — Institution License (No Branding)
- Bulk seat licenses for a facility or school
- Students access the platform as-is (SPD Cert Prep brand)
- Admin dashboard to track cohort progress
- **Pricing:** $8–$12/seat/month (volume discount), minimum 10 seats

#### Tier 2 — Co-Branded
- Facility logo added to header/footer
- Custom welcome message and onboarding
- Cohort management dashboard for instructors
- **Pricing:** $15–$20/seat/month + $500 setup fee, minimum 25 seats

#### Tier 3 — Full White-Label
- Custom subdomain (e.g., `prep.yourschool.edu`)
- Full logo/color replacement
- Optional custom question sets (facility-specific protocols)
- SSO integration (SAML/OAuth) with hospital identity systems
- Dedicated support
- **Pricing:** $2,000–$5,000/month flat fee OR $20–$30/seat/month, minimum 50 seats

---

### Technical Implementation Plan

#### Phase 1 — Multi-Tenant Foundation (2–3 weeks)

The current architecture (Supabase + Next.js) can support multi-tenancy with targeted changes:

1. **Add `organization_id` to Supabase schema:**
   - New `organizations` table: `id`, `name`, `slug`, `logo_url`, `primary_color`, `domain`, `plan_tier`, `seat_limit`, `created_at`
   - Add `organization_id` FK to `profiles` table
   - Row-level security policies scoped to organization

2. **Theming system:**
   - Move hardcoded brand colors (navy, teal, amber) to CSS variables
   - Load org-specific theme at runtime from Supabase config
   - Store logo URL in org record, render dynamically in header

3. **Subdomain routing in Next.js:**
   - Read hostname in middleware (`middleware.ts`)
   - Map subdomain to `organization_id` via database lookup
   - Inject org context into all page renders via server-side props

#### Phase 2 — Admin & Cohort Dashboard (2–3 weeks)

4. **Instructor/Admin portal (`/admin` route, org-scoped):**
   - Invite students via email (bulk CSV upload)
   - View cohort progress: domains mastered, readiness scores, time spent
   - Export reports (CSV/PDF) for compliance documentation
   - Set exam deadline dates per cohort

5. **Org-level billing:**
   - Stripe subscription with quantity-based billing
   - Seat management (add/remove users)
   - Invoice delivery to billing contact

#### Phase 3 — Custom Content (Optional, 4–6 weeks)

6. **Custom question sets:**
   - Org admins can add facility-specific questions (e.g., their instrument tray protocols)
   - Questions tagged by org and only shown to that org's users
   - AI chat context can include org-specific policy documents (via RAG)

7. **SSO / SAML integration:**
   - Integrate with common hospital SSO providers (Okta, Azure AD)
   - Use NextAuth.js or Supabase Auth SAML for implementation

### Go-To-Market for White-Label

#### Ideal First Customers
- Vocational/community colleges with SPD programs (search CAAHEP-accredited programs)
- Hospital systems with high SPD turnover rates or recent Joint Commission citations
- Large surgical centers and ASCs onboarding new SPD staff

#### Sales Motion
1. **Identify:** Search LinkedIn for "Sterile Processing Manager", "SPD Director", "Central Service Educator"
2. **Outreach:** Short LinkedIn message or cold email — lead with the compliance/liability angle ("Are your SPD techs certified? We help hospitals track and accelerate certification.")
3. **Demo:** 30-minute screen share showing the admin dashboard and student progress view
4. **Pilot:** Offer a 30-day free pilot for 10 seats — no commitment, just results
5. **Close:** Monthly contract, auto-renew, cancel anytime (removes friction)

#### Pricing Anchors for Sales Conversations
- "One failed Joint Commission audit costs more than a year of this platform."
- "Replacing one SPD tech costs $3,000–$8,000 in recruiting and training. Certification retention reduces turnover."
- "Your techs are studying on their phones anyway — give them a tool that tracks it."

#### Partnership Channels
- **HSPA / IAHCSMM:** Sponsor a chapter meeting or webinar; announce white-label option
- **Healthcare L&D conferences:** ASHHRA, ANCC, AORN Annual Congress
- **Aseptic Technical Solutions:** Already positioned as a facility training partner — natural upsell from in-person training to ongoing digital prep
