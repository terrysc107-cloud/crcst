# SPD Cert Prep — Product Roadmap

> Living document. Updated as priorities shift. Phases are directional, not locked.

---

## Where We Are Now (Baseline)

- 3 certifications: CRCST, CHL, CER — 700+ exam-aligned questions
- Free / Pro ($19 / 90 days) / Triple Crown ($39 / 90 days) tiers
- AI Study Chat (Claude-powered), Progression Mode (5 levels), streak tracking, digital badges
- Institutional access code system + wholesale admin dashboard
- Partner: Aseptic Technical Solutions (training programs)
- Stack: Next.js, Supabase, Vercel, Stripe

---

## Phase 1 — Solidify (Now → 3 months)

**Goal: Revenue validation + product stability**

### Product
- [ ] Monitor and resolve any Vercel/Supabase reliability issues
- [ ] Improve onboarding — capture exam date at sign-up, use it to drive urgency messaging
- [ ] Email study reminders (streak at risk, 2 weeks before exam date)
- [ ] Deliver SJT exam content (already in marketing pill — needs to ship)

### Revenue
- [ ] Close 3–5 institutional deals (hospitals, training programs, vocational schools)
- [ ] Build self-serve wholesale flow so orgs can buy seats without manual outreach
- [ ] Test annual + team pricing options alongside 90-day individual plans

### Metrics to Track
- Free → paid conversion rate
- Daily active users / streak retention rate
- Institutional pipeline value

---

## Phase 2 — Grow (3 → 9 months)

**Goal: Predictable acquisition + first $10k MRR**

### Product
- [ ] Mobile PWA with push notifications (streaks, exam countdown, daily reminders)
- [ ] Expand question bank to 1,000+ questions per certification
- [ ] Full-length timed mock exams (exam-day simulation mode)
- [ ] CEU tracking module (compliance requirement for many working techs)
- [ ] Peer leaderboards and cohort study groups for institutional users

### Acquisition
- [ ] Affiliate/referral program — instructors and SPD managers refer students, earn per activation
- [ ] SEO content strategy — "CRCST exam questions," "how to pass CHL," etc.
- [ ] Co-branded landing pages with Aseptic Technical Solutions
- [ ] LinkedIn and Facebook targeting for sterile processing professionals

### Institutional
- [ ] Self-serve org portal — admins buy seats, manage codes, view dashboards independently
- [ ] SCORM export of question sets for Canvas/Blackboard LMS integration

---

## Phase 3 — Scale (9 → 18 months)

**Goal: Dominant market position in SPD certification — $50k+ MRR**

### Product
- [ ] White-label deployments — hospital systems and schools get branded instances
- [ ] Instructor CMS — partner schools upload custom question sets and study materials
- [ ] Adaptive learning engine — AI builds personalized daily study plans from gap analysis
- [ ] Post-certification path — continuing ed, career tools, job matching

### Institutional
- [ ] Enterprise contracts with hospital networks (multi-facility licensing)
- [ ] Vocational school curriculum integration — accredited programs adopt platform as coursework
- [ ] Pursue IAHCSMM or CBSPD official/recommended study partner designation

### Revenue Model Evolution
- Individual: shift from one-time 90-day to monthly/annual SaaS options
- Institutional: per-seat annual contracts + platform fee
- White-label: setup fee + monthly platform license

---

## Phase 4 — Platform (18 → 36 months)

**Goal: Healthcare credentialing platform beyond SPD**

- [ ] Expand to adjacent certifications (pharmacy tech, surgical tech, central supply)
- [ ] API access for third-party LMS and HR system integrations
- [ ] Employer credentialing verification — verify certification status directly through platform
- [ ] International expansion — WFHSS-aligned content for UK, Canada, Australia
- [ ] Community layer — SPD professional network, mentorship, job board

---

## What Stays Flexible

- Certification expansion order — follow demand signals, don't guess
- Pricing model — test before committing to SaaS shift
- White-label vs. hosted institutional model — let early customers tell you which they want
- Mobile — native app vs. PWA depends on actual usage data
- International timing — validate US market fully before expanding

---

## Strategic Priority (The Unlock)

**Official or semi-official partnership with IAHCSMM or CBSPD.**

A recommended study resource designation or co-branded partner status would:
- Drop individual acquisition costs dramatically
- Accelerate institutional deal cycles
- Create a defensible moat against future competitors

Worth pursuing in parallel with all phases above.
