# White-Label Outreach Plan — Schools & Hospitals

## Who to Target First

### Tier A — Easiest to Close (Start Here)

**Community colleges & vocational schools with SPD programs**
- They have a captive audience (enrolled students) and a clear need
- Budget is small but decision is made by one person (program director)
- CAAHEP accredits SPD programs — this is your prospect list

  **How to find them:**
  - Google: `site:caahep.org "sterile processing"` — pulls accredited programs
  - Google: `"sterile processing technician program" community college`
  - Indeed/LinkedIn: search job title "Sterile Processing Program Director" or "Central Service Instructor"

**Independent SPD training schools**
- Private schools that run 4–8 week accelerated SPD certification courses
- They charge students $800–$2,500 for the course and need exam prep materials
- Currently handing out photocopied study sheets or pointing students to Amazon textbooks
- Your platform is a direct upgrade at a fraction of the cost

  **How to find them:**
  - Google: `sterile processing certification training school [city]`
  - Search Groupon/CourseHorse — SPD bootcamps are listed there
  - Facebook: search "sterile processing training" — many run Facebook pages

---

### Tier B — Bigger Deal, Longer Sales Cycle

**Hospital systems with high SPD headcount**
- Large hospital networks (HCA, Tenet, CommonSpirit, Trinity Health, Ascension) employ hundreds of SPD techs across facilities
- One deal = 50–200+ seats
- Decision maker: Director of Sterile Processing, VP of Surgical Services, or L&D/Education department

  **How to find them:**
  - LinkedIn: search `"Sterile Processing Manager" OR "SPD Director" OR "Central Service Director"` — filter by hospital/health system
  - Hospital websites: look under "Surgical Services" or "Perioperative" leadership
  - HSPA member directory (if you join as a vendor member)

**Staffing agencies placing SPD techs**
- Agencies like Aya Healthcare, Maxim, AMN Healthcare place travel SPD techs
- They want their candidates to arrive certified — your platform is a pre-placement prep tool
- One agency relationship = recurring referrals across their entire SPD candidate pool

## The Outreach Sequence

### For Schools (Program Directors)

**Day 1 — LinkedIn connection request + message**

Keep it short. Don't pitch in the first message.

> Hi [Name] — I noticed you run the sterile processing program at [School]. I'm building a digital exam prep tool specifically for CRCST/CHL/CER — curious if you'd find a 15-minute call useful to see if it fits your students. No pressure either way.

**Day 4 — Follow-up if no reply**

> Hey [Name] — just bumping this up. Happy to send over a free demo access link if easier than a call.

**Day 9 — Final follow-up**

> Last note — I'll leave you alone after this. If exam prep tools ever come up for your SPD students, feel free to reach back out. Here's our site: [link]

## The Demo Call — What to Show (15 Minutes)

**Minutes 0–2: Qualify**
- "How many SPD techs do you have on staff?"
- "What percentage are currently CRCST certified?"
- "How are they studying for the exam right now?"

**Minutes 2–10: Show the platform**
- Student view: custom quiz builder, domain mastery tracker, AI chat (answer a real question live)
- Admin view: show cohort progress dashboard mockup or describe it — "you'd see every tech's readiness score by domain, so you know who needs more time before exam day"
- Certification badge: "when they pass, they get a digital badge they can share on LinkedIn"

**Minutes 10–13: Address the obvious objections**
- *"We don't have budget"* → "Our pilot is free — 30 days, no cost, no contract. You only pay if you want to keep it."
- *"Our staff studies on their own time"* → "Exactly why it's self-paced and mobile-friendly. They can do 10 questions between cases."
- *"We use [textbook / other tool]"* → "This works alongside it — we're the practice layer, not a replacement for the textbook."

**Minutes 13–15: Close the pilot**
- "Can I set up a free 30-day pilot for your team? I just need a list of email addresses and I'll have everyone activated by end of week."

## Pricing to Quote in Outreach

Keep it simple in first conversations — don't lead with price, lead with the pilot.

| When they ask | What to say |
|---|---|
| Schools (per cohort) | "We typically do $8–$12 per student per month. For a cohort of 20, that's under $200/month." |
| Hospital (per seat) | "$12–$15 per tech per month. For 50 techs, that's $600–$750/month — less than one failed certification retake fee." |
| Hospital (flat rate) | "For larger teams we can do a flat $1,500–$2,000/month for unlimited seats in your facility." |
| Price objection | "The pilot is free. Let's see if your staff actually uses it first." |

## Where to Find Prospect Lists Fast

### LinkedIn (Best Source)

Search these titles, filter by "Healthcare" or "Hospital & Health Care" industry:
- `Sterile Processing Manager`
- `SPD Director`
- `Central Service Director`
- `Sterile Processing Educator`
- `Sterile Processing Program Director`
- `Surgical Services Director`

Build a list of 50 names/week. Send connection requests with the short message above. Takes 30–45 minutes.

### HSPA (Healthcare Sterile Processing Association)

- Join as a vendor/associate member (~$300/year)
- Access to member directory, chapter meetings, and the annual conference
- One HSPA chapter presentation = 20–50 warm leads in the room

### Google Maps + Phone

For training schools:
- Google Maps: search `sterile processing training` in major cities (NYC, LA, Chicago, Houston, Atlanta, Dallas)
- Call the school directly, ask for the program director
- This is faster than LinkedIn for small private schools that aren't active online

### Indeed / Job Boards (Reverse Signal)

Hospitals posting for "SPD Educator" or "Central Service Trainer" are actively building out training infrastructure — they're warm leads. Search Indeed for those titles and reach out to the hiring manager.
